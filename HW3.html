About Us

The California State University, Northridge (CSUN) College of Engineering and Computer Science is dedicated to providing a broad, rigorous, application-oriented, and contemporary understanding of engineering. This is why they offer the engineering students at CSUN the invaluable opportunity to participate in the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition (IGVC) as an option to fulfill their senior design project requirements. Participation in the program will enhance the knowledge, creativity, and imagination of the team members and prepare them for successful careers and lifelong learning. The CSUN Intelligent Ground Vehicle (IGV) Team requests your investment in an educational program which offers a design experience that is at the very cutting edge of engineering education.

The purpose of the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition (IGVC) is to design and assemble a fully autonomous unmanned ground robotic vehicle that can negotiate a course with obstacles and perform tasks assigned to it during the competition. The goals of this competition are to advance the state-of-the-art of intelligent ground vehicles (IGVs) by challenging a new generation of engineers to perform realistic missions on the competition course and to foster ties between young engineers and the organizations developing IGV technologies.

We won the IGVC competition in 2012! Our robot, Red Raven 2.0, secured the overall championship at the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition, while our second robot, LINJA, earned third place in the design competition. This success reflects the team's commitment to excellence and innovation in engineering.

We ranked 7th in the IGVC competition in 2024! Dr. Vidya Nandikolla and her team of students made a remarkable comeback after a decade without prior design information, standing out among 29 universities from Egypt, Canada, India, and the USA. This achievement highlights the team's dedication to excellence in engineering and innovation.

We are also pursuing new advancements in intelligent ground vehicles, focusing on cutting-edge technologies and methodologies to prepare our team for future challenges and competitions. We will be participating in the 2025 IGVC, so stay tuned for more updates!
